Step-by-Step Guide

Application Protocol

Lumina Smooth Therapy

A complete professional protocol for applying Lumina Smooth Therapy. Follow each phase precisely to deliver alignment, control, and lasting shine without compromising hair health.

For Licensed Professionals Only. Always perform a strand and patch test before full application. Use gloves. Ensure salon ventilation. Lumina is a chemical hair-fiber treatment intended exclusively for trained cosmetologists.

01

Phase One

Preparation & Sectioning

  • Wash strands with Deep Clean clarifying shampoo or Detox Therapy
  • Towel-dry to remove excess water
  • Divide hair into four quadrants
  • Section ear to ear
  • Organize top, sides, and nape
02

Phase Two

Application

  • Apply product 1 cm from the root
  • Distribute with a comb through to the ends
  • Work in fine sections
  • Lift each section to 90°
  • Avoid contact with the scalp
Note Direct contact with the scalp can cause irritation. Maintain the 1 cm distance throughout.
03

Phase Three

Alignment & Pause Time

  • Keep hair aligned at all times
  • Apply along the natural fall of the hair
  • At the top, maintain a backward direction
  • If needed, lightly mist with water — aids product conduction and absorption
  • Ensure all hair is fully saturated
  • Action time: 30 to 60 minutes, depending on hair density
  • Do not tie up or twist the strands
04

Phase Four

Client Comfort & Rinse

  • In case of tingling, clean the area with damp cotton
  • Do not scratch the scalp
  • A full rinse is not required
  • Remove between 50% and 80% of the product
  • For blonde hair: remove 100%
05

Phase Five

Drying & Thermal Activation

  • Dry hair 100%
  • Blow-dry with a polished, smooth brush finish
  • Flat-iron in fine sections
  • Lift each section to 90°
  • 10 to 20 passes per section
  • Average temperature: 160°C to 230°C / 320°F to 446°F

Thermal Control

Temperature by Hair Type

Fine & Fragile

160°C – 180°C 320°F – 356°F

Thick & Resistant

200°C – 230°C 392°F – 446°F

  • Do not fold or twist the section
  • Avoid repeating heat in the same spot
  • On blondes, use caution with heat — excess can cause marking and color shift
06

Phase Six

Final Rinse & ACID BONDING

  • Apply ACID BONDING acidifier or FUSION CMC Step 2
  • Leave on for 3 minutes, then rinse
  • Apply FUSION CMC Step 3
  • Dry without brushing
07

Phase Seven

Client Aftercare

  • Dry hair while keeping it aligned after each wash
  • Repeat the directional flow of the strands
  • Maintain a regular care routine — extends the alignment effect
