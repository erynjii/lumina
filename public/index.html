About Lumina by Mirra Professional

Lumina is an advanced professional hair care line by Mirra Professional, a Brazilian cosmetics manufacturer. The Lumina line features two flagship products: Lumina Smooth Therapy (1000ml) for professional hair smoothing using aminothiol technology, and Lumina Tint Hair (60ml) for blonde and highlighted hair color maintenance.

Lumina Smooth Therapy - Professional Hair Smoothing Treatment

Lumina Smooth Therapy is a next-generation hair smoothing treatment that uses aminothiol agents for internal hair fiber restructuring. It delivers real alignment, volume control, frizz elimination, flexibility, natural movement, and premium shine. Safe for all hair types. SKU: 52749. Size: 1000ml / 33.8 fl oz. Made in Brazil.

Lumina Tint Hair - Color Maintenance Serum

Lumina Tint Hair is a professional-grade color maintenance serum for blonde and highlighted hair. Key ingredients include Allium Cepa Bulb Extract, Hydrolyzed Corn Protein, Cysteine HCl, and Cysteamine HCl. It shields against heat-induced oxidation and neutralizes yellow and brassy tones. SKU: 52750. Size: 60ml / 2.02 fl oz. Made in Brazil.
